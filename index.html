About Little Joints Therapy Care

Dedicated to compassionate, person‑centered care to help every individual move better and live pain‑free.

At Little Joints Therapy Care, we help children and adults recover, reduce pain, and regain confidence in movement. Our mission is to provide individualized physiotherapy and rehabilitation that’s effective, empathetic, and easy to follow.

We use evidence‑based methods tailored to your goals — from sports injuries and postural concerns to pain conditions including knee pain, shoulder pain, back pain, and more. Together, we build a plan that fits your daily life and delivers lasting results.

Child-Centered

Every treatment plan is designed around your child's unique needs, interests, and developmental stage.

Evidence-Based Methods

We use the latest research and proven techniques to ensure the most effective treatment outcomes.

Gentle & Playful

Our approach makes therapy fun and engaging, helping children look forward to their sessions.
